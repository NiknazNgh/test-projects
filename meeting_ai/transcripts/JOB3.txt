GENERAL SUMMARY 05/02/2025 Performs as an experienced technical liaison for business applications and project analyst for assigned area. Administers and maintains projects, systems and/or technical business processes. Provides user support and helps to identify customer needs and improve business processes. Serves as a point of contact for issues related to departmental systems, training, procedures and/or testing. ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES The intent of this job description is to provide a representative summary of the major duties and responsibilities performed by incumbents of this job. Incumbents may be requested to perform job-related tasks other than those specifically presented in this description. 1. Plans, tracks and oversees activities on assigned departmental projects related to systems and business requirements. 2. Consults with the IT department when necessary to design and document basic work flows to ensure proper application/user interface; may create document links. 4. Uses and/or troubleshoots related software (e.g. GIS, Accela, ILS, TASKE, NICE, CODE-Ops, etc.). 8. May configure applications interfaces to include department dashboards. 10. Tracks and oversees the procurement process for software and/or hardware and maintains department inventory. 11. Serves as technical and systems liaison to other departments and outside vendors. 12. Performs other related duties as required. 13.
